Ready to join our vibrant team at Bonbon Tours?

We're constantly on the lookout for passionate guides to become a part of our expanding, diverse team and offer travelers a truly unique experience of Paris.

Are you someone who breathes art, history, and culture? Do you possess an intimate knowledge of the city or its charming neighborhoods? If you're eager to share your expertise with travelers, then your profile could be exactly what we're looking for.

Here's what you can expect when working with us:

  • Flexibility: Work as little or as much as you prefer. While we appreciate long-term cooperation, we value guides we can rely on year-round.
  • Independence: Craft your own tour narrative within the general structure provided. It's your chance to get creative.
  • Availability: Provide your schedule at least a month in advance, and we'll allocate tours based on your availability.
  • Booking System: Enjoy the ease of a streamlined booking system, eliminating the need for complicated clipboards and paperwork.
  • Protocol: While we don't enforce many rules, guides are expected to use designated software and follow the operational protocol provided by Bonbon Tours.

The Ideal Candidate:

Confident, outgoing, and dynamic with excellent communication skills.

Strong crowd management skills, capable of leading groups effectively.

Fluent in English; additional languages are a bonus.

Reliable, punctual, and able to work independently.

Problem solver with the ability to handle diverse client expectations.

Enjoys engaging with people from different backgrounds in a bustling environment.

Keen to improve knowledge about tours and takes the initiative for independent research.

Possesses a charming sense of humor that captivates diverse audiences.

General Requirements:

Fluent in English, as the majority of our tours are conducted in English.

Legal work authorization as a freelancer in Paris.

Previous experience in independent work or university.

In-depth knowledge of the chosen neighborhood or monuments.

Flexible availability and adaptability.

Proficient in smartphone and computer skills.

Selection Process:

Send your CV, photograph, and a brief cover letter explaining why we should collaborate.

Include your best and worst guiding reviews, along with a list of tours you already know and those you're in the process of learning. (optional)

If your application stands out, we'll reach out for a short interview.

Ready to embark on this exciting journey with Bonbon Tours? Email all your information to toursbonbon@gmail.com
